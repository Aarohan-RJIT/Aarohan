Solo Dance

When we hear the word "dancing," the first thing that springs to mind is movement and steps to the rhythm of the music. Dancing is an uplifting activity. A solo dance is one that is performed by just one person. You'll have the chance to show off your dance prowess and your talents with Aarohan.

Group Dance

Group dances are choreographed dances that are performed simultaneously by three or more people in a group. It also builds bonds of friendship and excitement among those involved. Aarohan offers you the chance to demonstrate your ability to coordinate your steps with others in a group dance, as well as the chance to have a lot of fun and excitement.

Singing & Rapping

Making musical tones with your voice is the action of singing.
As opposed to that, rhyme, rhythmic speech, and street jargon are all used in the musical form of vocal delivery known as rapping. Rap includes "content," "flow," and "delivery" as its constituent parts. You have the opportunity to present your singing or rapping abilities with Aarohan.

Stand-Up & Open Mic

A comic tells their own jokes live on stage in front of a live audience as part of a stand-up comedy show or performance.
An open mic is a live performance at a location where anyone is welcome to perform on stage in a variety of ways, including telling jokes, reading poetry, singing, etc. Arohan allows you to perform on stage, showcase your comedic or other abilities, and please the audience.
