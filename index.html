ABOUT US

Rustamji Institute of Technology (RJIT) is the first engineering college established by Paramilitary Force having a history of 24 years, affiliated by RGPV Bhopal.
The Institute is named after the founding father of BSF Padma Vibhushan late K.F Rustamji. The aim for setting up the institute was to provide the technical education for the children of BSF personnels who are in military affected area and are unable to look after education of their children.
The institute encourages the message of
"आत्म दीपो भव:" , "Be Your Own Light"

With great excitement, we present to you the forthcoming three-day fête named Aarohan. This celebratory extravaganza promises to be a harmonious amalgamation of Technical, Non-Technical, and Cultural events.
